Privacy Policy

Camera Scanner · by OWESI STUDIO · Last updated: June 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how OWESI STUDIO ("we", "us") handles information in the Camera Scanner mobile application (the "App"). By using the App you agree to this policy.

1. The short version

  • We do not collect your name, photos, or videos.
  • Camera frames are processed on your device and are never stored or uploaded.
  • We use anonymous analytics and crash reports to improve the App.
  • The App shows ads (Google AdMob and mediation partners), which may use an advertising identifier.
  • Your scan history is stored only on your phone and you can clear it anytime.

2. Information we collect

We do not ask you to create an account or provide personal details. The App may process:

  • Usage & analytics data (via Google Firebase Analytics): anonymous events such as which detector you open, scans started, and screens viewed, plus device model and Android version. This helps us improve the App.
  • Crash data (via Google Firebase Crashlytics): diagnostic logs if the App crashes.
  • Advertising data (via Google AdMob and mediation partners — Meta Audience Network and Pangle): your device's advertising identifier, IP address, and general device information, used to show and measure ads. In the EEA/UK we ask for your consent first through Google's consent form (UMP).

3. Device permissions and why we use them

  • Camera — to show a live preview for the Infrared and Lens detectors. Frames are analysed on-device in real time and are never saved or transmitted.
  • Location (Android 12 and below only) — Android requires location permission to read nearby Wi-Fi networks during a wireless scan. We do not track or store your location.
  • Nearby Wi-Fi devices / Bluetooth — to detect nearby wireless and Bluetooth cameras.
  • Notifications — to alert you about scan results.

No photos, videos, audio, or scan imagery ever leave your device.

4. Data stored on your device

Your scan history and app settings are saved locally on your phone only. They are not uploaded to us. You can delete your history at any time from the History screen ("Clear All").

5. Third-party services

The App relies on the following third parties, each with its own privacy policy:

  • Google AdMob & Firebase — policies.google.com/privacy
  • Meta Audience Network — facebook.com/about/privacy
  • Pangle — pangleglobal.com/privacy

6. Children's privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

7. Your rights

Depending on your region (e.g. GDPR in the EEA/UK, CCPA in California) you may request access to or deletion of data associated with you, and you can withdraw ad-consent at any time. Because we do not hold account data, most requests relate to advertising identifiers managed in your device settings. Contact us for any request.

8. Data security

We use reasonable technical measures to protect data handled by the App. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but on-device processing keeps your camera and scan data private by design.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date above reflects the latest version.

10. Contact us

Questions about this policy? Email info@owesistudio.com or visit owesistudio.com.
