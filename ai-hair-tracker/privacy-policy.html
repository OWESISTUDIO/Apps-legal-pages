3. How your photos are handled

Your privacy around photos is central to the App:

  • Your photos are kept on your device.
  • To analyse a scan, the photos are sent securely over an encrypted connection to our AI processing service, used only to produce your results, and are not stored on our servers afterwards.
  • The numerical results of an analysis (e.g. scores and dates) are saved so you can view your history and progress.
  • You can delete your on-device photos and your account data at any time from within the App (Settings → Your Data Rights).

4. How we use your information

  • Provide the core features: AI hair analysis, scoring, progress tracking, and a personalised routine.
  • Create and secure your account and authenticate you via Google Sign-In.
  • Process and verify subscriptions and manage the free-scan allowance.
  • Show interstitial ads to free users (Premium users are not shown ads).
  • Diagnose crashes and maintain stability, security, and fraud/abuse prevention.
  • Understand aggregate feature usage to improve the App (only where analytics is enabled).

5. Legal bases (GDPR / UK GDPR)

Where the EU/UK GDPR applies, we rely on: performance of a contract (to provide the App and your account), consent (e.g. optional analytics and, where required, personalised ads), and legitimate interests (security, abuse prevention, and keeping the App stable). You can withdraw consent at any time.

7. Advertising (Google AdMob)

  • Free users may be shown a full-screen (interstitial) ad after generating a scan result. Premium subscribers are not shown ads.
  • Ads are served by Google AdMob, which may use your device’s advertising identifier. See Google’s policies: How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services.
  • You can reset or limit your advertising ID in your device settings (Settings → Google → Ads), or upgrade to Premium to remove ads.

8. Analytics & crash reporting

  • Analytics (Firebase Analytics) is used to understand aggregate, non-identifying usage. You can turn it off at any time in Settings → Privacy & Data → Analytics.
  • Crash reporting (Firebase Crashlytics) is used to keep the App stable. Crash reports are tied only to a Firebase account identifier and do not include your email, name, or photos.

9. Data retention

We keep your account and analysis data for as long as your account exists. Photos are not retained on our servers after an analysis. When you delete your account (Settings → Your Data Rights → Delete Account), your associated data is deleted from your device and from our systems, except where we must retain limited records to meet legal, security, or accounting obligations.

Depending on your location (including under GDPR/UK GDPR and CCPA), you may have the right to:

  • Withdraw consent (e.g. turn off analytics) and object to certain processing;
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data-protection authority.

We do not sell or “share” personal information for cross-context behavioural advertising as defined under CCPA.

11. Data security

We use industry-standard safeguards, including encryption of data in transit (TLS), app-integrity verification (Firebase App Check), and account-based access controls. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we work to protect your information.

12. International data transfers

13. Children’s privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 (or under 16 where applicable in the EEA). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us and we will delete it.
