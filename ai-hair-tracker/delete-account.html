Delete Your Account & Data

Application: AI Hair Tracker  •  Developer: Owesi Studio  •  Package: com.owesistudio.hairtracker

Last updated: 20 July 2026

You can permanently delete your AI Hair Tracker account and its associated data at any time. You do not need to reinstall the app to make this request — the email option below works even if you have already uninstalled it. There are two ways to delete your account:

Option A — Delete from inside the app (fastest)

If you still have the app installed:

  1. Open AI Hair Tracker and go to Settings.
  2. Tap Delete Account.
  3. Confirm the deletion. Your account and data are removed right away.

Option B — Request deletion by email (no app needed)

If you have uninstalled the app or prefer to request it here, send us a deletion request using the button below. This is the recommended path for anyone who no longer has the app.

✉️ Request Account Deletion

Or email us directly at info@owesistudio.com and include the details below.

Information to include (so we can find your account)

Please provide only what is needed to identify your account:

Subject: Account Deletion Request - AI Hair Tracker Google Sign-In email: <the email you used to sign in> User ID (optional): <only if you know it> Confirmation: I confirm I want my account and associated data permanently deleted.

We use the email address you signed in with (Google Sign-In) to locate your account. Please send the request from — or clearly state — that same email so we can verify ownership.

What gets deleted

When your request is processed, we permanently delete:

  • Account information — your app account and profile details.
  • Saved scan results — your captured photos and their analyses.
  • Hair analysis history — density scores, trends, estimates, and dates.
  • Associated user data — in-app preferences, settings, and usage records tied to your account.

This removes your data both from our servers and, where applicable, from the app on your device.

What we may retain (and why)

After deletion, we keep only limited records where we are required or permitted to, for legal, security, fraud-prevention, billing, or accounting purposes. These are kept for no longer than necessary:

Retained recordPurpose
Purchase / subscription transaction records Legal, tax, and accounting obligations. Payment and billing records are held by Google Play; we do not store your payment details.
Anti-fraud purchase reference (a one-way token reference) Security and fraud prevention — to stop a single purchase from being reused or abused across accounts.
Aggregated / anonymized diagnostics App stability and analytics. This data is not linked to your identity after deletion.

For full details of how we handle and retain data, see our Privacy Policy.

Cancel your subscription separately

Important: Deleting your account does not automatically cancel an active Google Play subscription. If you have an AI Hair Tracker subscription, please cancel it separately in Google Play → Subscriptions (or at play.google.com/store/account/subscriptions) to avoid further charges.

How long it takes

We process account deletion requests within 30 days. In some cases, residual copies in secure backups are fully purged within 90 days. We will confirm by email once your request is complete.
